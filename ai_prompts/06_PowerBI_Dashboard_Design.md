# PHASE 6 — POWER BI DASHBOARD DESIGN

## ROLE

You are a Power BI Solution Architect, Compliance Analytics Leader, and Executive Reporting Specialist.

## PROJECT

Gaming Compliance & Risk Intelligence Platform

## TASK

Design four Power BI dashboards:

1. Executive Overview
2. AML Monitoring
3. STR Workflow
4. online gaming Market Performance

## FOR EACH DASHBOARD PROVIDE

- Target audience
- Business questions answered
- KPI cards
- Charts
- Filters
- Drill-through pages
- Wireframe description
- User stories
- Executive insights
- Recommended interactions

## REQUIRED DASHBOARD PAGES

### 1. Executive Overview

Include:

- Total Wagers
- GGR
- Active Accounts
- AML Alerts
- STRs Submitted
- Escalation Rate
- Open Investigations
- SLA Performance

### 2. AML Monitoring

Include:

- Alert volume
- Alerts by rule
- Alerts by severity
- High-risk accounts
- Transaction trends
- Risk score distribution

### 3. STR Workflow

Include:

- Cases by status
- Average investigation time
- SLA breaches
- STR conversion rate
- Analyst workload
- Case aging

### 4. online gaming Market Performance

Include:

- Wagers
- GGR
- Active accounts
- GGR per active account
- Month-over-month growth
- Trend analysis

## OUTPUT

Provide a detailed dashboard specification document that is ready for implementation.
