# PHASE 1 — ARCHITECTURE BLUEPRINT

## ROLE

You are a Principal Data Architect, Senior AML Compliance Manager, online gaming Risk Consultant, Regulatory Reporting Specialist, and Business Intelligence Director.

## PROJECT

Gaming Compliance & Risk Intelligence Platform

## PROJECT PURPOSE

This project serves as a portfolio-quality demonstration of skills relevant to:

- AML Analyst
- Fraud Analyst
- Compliance Analyst
- Regulatory Reporting Analyst
- online gaming Compliance Specialist
- Risk & Controls Analyst

The project must simulate a realistic regulated online gaming operator environment using publicly available or clearly labelled synthetic data.

## DATA SOURCES

Use ONLY:

1. IBM AMLSim
2. IBM AML-Data
3. AMLSim Example Dataset on Kaggle

## PROJECT SCOPE

The platform will eventually contain:

1. AML Transaction Monitoring
2. STR Workflow Management
3. online gaming GGR Reporting
4. Executive Compliance Reporting

## CRITICAL DEVELOPMENT CONSTRAINT

Do NOT design the entire solution at once.

Follow this lifecycle:

1. Architecture Blueprint
2. Data Model Design
3. SQL Database & Analytics Foundation
4. Jupyter Data Exploration
5. AML Monitoring Rules
6. STR Workflow
7. Power BI Reporting
8. Portfolio Documentation

Only complete Phase 1.

Do not generate SQL.
Do not generate Power BI.
Do not generate DAX.
Do not generate AML rules.

## TASK

Create the Architecture Blueprint.

Include:

- Business case
- Regulatory context
- Stakeholders
- Business objectives
- Compliance objectives
- AML objectives
- Success metrics
- Risks
- Assumptions
- High-level architecture
- Data flow
- Technology stack
- Project roadmap
- Deliverables by phase

## OUTPUT FORMAT

Provide:

1. Executive Summary
2. Business Context
3. Architecture Overview
4. Stakeholder Analysis
5. Project Roadmap
6. Risks & Assumptions
7. Recommended Next Phase

The output should resemble a consulting deliverable that would be presented to senior management before any development begins.
