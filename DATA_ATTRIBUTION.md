# Data Attribution

## Synthetic data — all datasets in this repository

Every dataset shipped here is **synthetic**, generated by
`data_raw/synthetic_data_generator.py` with a deterministic seed and clearly
labelled as such.

### AML transactions & STR cases
- Mirrors the IBM *Transactions for AML* schema so a real dataset can be substituted.
- Contains **no real customer information**; all accounts, players, analysts, cases,
  and the sanctions/PEP flags are fabricated.

### Market / GGR series
- A fabricated monthly online-gaming market series (`market_monthly_synthetic.csv`,
  `market_by_product_synthetic.csv`): cash wagers, GGR, active accounts, ARPPA, and a
  product-category split over 48 synthetic months.
- Figures are **illustrative only** and do **not** correspond to any real market,
  operator, or regulator.

## Reference datasets (not redistributed here)

- IBM *Transactions for AML* / AMLSim — referenced only as the schema basis; not included.

## Disclaimer

This is an independent portfolio project built entirely on synthetic and public,
open-source data. It is **not affiliated with, endorsed by, or representative of**
any regulator, gaming authority, or operator, and uses **no proprietary or internal
company information**. All figures are fabricated for demonstration.
